Shuffle Trance

About

Shuffle Trance is a simple game where your main objective is to keep the ball bouncing, every time your ball bounces with something, you'll score points, as you keep scoring, your score multiplier will increase, which will make you score even more points with every bounce.

As you play, you'll see different obstacles and power-ups that will either make the game easier or harder, every time your ball collides with a star, the style of the game will change completely, making this a unique game, there are billions of possible styles so the game will remain fresh and original.

Power-Ups

Bigger Ball

Makes bigger a ball when there is a collision

Bigger Paddle

Makes bigger your paddle when a ball collides with it

Bomb

Explodes and destroys near obstacles

Extra Ball

Gives you an extra ball

Gun

Gives you the avility to shoot 10 times by tapping

Extra Balls

Gives you 2 extra balls

Slow/Fast

Makes the game faster or slower

Smaller Ball

Makes smaller a ball when there is a collision

Smaller Paddle

Makes smaller your paddle when a ball collides with it

Game developed by Daniel Fernandez Arrieta
