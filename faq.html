Frequently Asqued Questions

Q: How many styles are there?

A: It's hard to answer since every time there is an update there are millions of new styles added, but as for now it's calculated that there are over 600,000,000,000 distinguishable styles

Q: How is it possible to have so many styles?
A: The styles you see in game are not stored locally, they are code generated live as you play, making it possible to have that impresive amount of styles

Q: Will I ever see all of the styles?

A: It's quite unlikely

Q: How do I get to the secret sreen?

A: You can tap your on the home screen with 3 fingers, or if you're using a Mac, you can type the leter "w" on your keyboard

Q: I like my current style, how can I keep it for later?

A: You can tap on your screen with 2 fingers or if you're using a Mac, you can type the leter "m" on your keyboard, this will save the current style to your clipboard, you can paste it wherever you want, even share it with friends!

Q: How can I get back a style I've saved?

A: Go to the secret screen, there you can paste or type a style you like

Q: I love my current style, how can I use it as a background?

A: If you are using an iPhone or iPad, you can go to the secret screen and type the code: background, this will generate a background image with your current style and save it to your pictures

Q: How can I get stars faster?

A: When you finish a level, you can always watch a rewarded video to get 15 extra stars, you can also purchase the double stars IAP to collect two stars every time you collet one

Q: How can I change my ball?

A: To change your ball, tap the shop icon, there you'll be able to purchase with your collected stars and use any balls you'd like, if you don't have enough stars, you can always purchase more as an IAP
